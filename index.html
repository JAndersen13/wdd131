About Arizona

Arizona is a beautiful state in the United States and is home to the Grand Canyon, a popular tourist destination. Arizona's landscape ranges from mountinous deserts, full of the tall saguaro cacti, to pine forests, majestic canyons, plains, and much more. Arizona's state flower is the saguaro cacti's flower, and to the surprise of many, it does in fact snow in Arizona. There are two major ski resorts, one in Flagstaff, and the other in the White Mountains. The summers in the Phoenix and Tuscon areas often reach well over 100 degrees Farenheit, and it isn't uncommon to see 120 degree days, to the dismay of all.
